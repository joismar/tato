Intelligent Mode Tutorial:

Intelligent mode automatically corrects the most common scores such as:

  • Capitalize the first letter of the words after punctuation and paragraph break.
  • Enter [space] after the comma.
  • In the case of proper nouns, the user has the option of declaring explicitly with the \# at the beginning of the word. Ex: #capitalize = Capitalize.
  • If the user types more than 3 capital letters in sequence within a word or the word is made up of 2 or more capital letters, they will not be capitalized.
